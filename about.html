Meet Melanie

The Cookie Edit was born from a simple but deeply held belief — that a cookie made with real, honest ingredients and genuine care can change the mood of an entire day.

No shortcuts. No mystery additives. Just the kind of ingredients you'd already find in your own pantry, handled with intention.

Melanie has loved baking and eating cookies her whole life. What started as a personal passion became something bigger — a chance to create moments of joy for other people. There's something about watching someone take that first bite and smile that never gets old.

The Cookie Edit is more than a cookie business. It's a pursuit of craft, a commitment to quality, and a reminder that the simplest things — done really well — matter the most.
