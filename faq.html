Pricing
Our pricing is simple — $4.00 per cookie, always. Individual cookies are available à la carte, or choose The Starter Box (3 curated cookies for $10) or The Dozen Box (12 cookies, your choice of flavors, for $39).
The Starter Box is our curated introduction to The Cookie Edit — 3 cookies for $10, featuring our Classic Chocolate Chip, M&M, and Triple Chocolate. It's the perfect way to try us for the first time or share with a friend.
The Cookies
We use real, honest ingredients — the kind you'd already find in your own pantry. Cold butter, brown sugar, eggs, vanilla, and quality chocolate. No artificial preservatives, no mystery additives. Just good ingredients handled with care.
All of our cookies contain wheat, eggs, and dairy. Our Salted Caramel cookie also contains tree nuts (almonds) via Heath toffee bits. All cookies are baked in the same kitchen, so cross-contact is possible. If you have a severe allergy, please reach out before ordering at hello@thecookieedit.com.
Yes — every order is baked to order. We never pre-bake and stockpile. Your cookies are made for you, for your pickup date, and that's it. That's how we keep quality exactly where it needs to be.
Our cookies are best enjoyed within 3 days of pickup, stored at room temperature in their packaging. If you need to keep them longer, they freeze beautifully — just seal in an airtight bag and thaw at room temperature when you're ready.
